### Utilities

* [QuickScan](https://github.com/raboof/realtimeconfigquickscan/)
  Linux configuration checker for systems to be used for real-time audio.
  Requires [perl-tk](https://www.archlinux.org/packages/extra/x86_64/perl-tk/)
  package.
